About
Maria
Ruelas

Maria Fernanda Ruelas Arceo was born in Mexico City, Mexico, and moved to New Hampshire at the age of 11. She graduated from Concord High School in 2023 and began her professional journey by enrolling at Syracuse University. Currently, she is pursuing a dual degree in Visual Communications and Information Management & Technology.

Maria is fluent in Spanish and has conversational French skills. Besides this, she is certified in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Furthermore, Maria has achieved Dean’s list and is the recipient of the Dean’s Scholarship and the Invest in Success Scholarship.

She is a very artistic individual who enjoys hobbies such as painting, crafting, taking pictures, and playing guitar, among many others. Maria loves to read fantasy novels in her free time and is very dedicated to working out and staying active. She enjoys exploring the world and going out, seeking new experiences.

Maria’s professional goals are to graduate with a Visual Communications major, with a concentration in Graphic Design, and an Information Management major, with a double concentration in Web Development and Project Management. Her goals after college are to pursue a career in UX/UI Design for a company based in New York City or Boston.
